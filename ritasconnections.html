HOW TO USE THE SEARCH INTERFACE

  1. For an EASY, QUALITY and PRECISE SEARCH, use NovaSeek™.

    Tap on THE RED NAVIGATION BUTTON in the TOP LEFT CORNER of the page and the page autoscrolls down to "Ladies' Profiles" for you. So, select your criteria in any or all of the boxes and the search results grid will fetch profiles that match your selection to the top of the results Immediately.

    So just go back to Profiles once you're done selecting.

  2. Profiles that match 4 of your WANTS will be listed first before profiles that match 3 are. And then 2, 1, and 0.

  3. Any result that doesn't match your LOCATION SELECTION will not be displayed. To get all results, keep it in ALL.

  4. For BASIC SEARCH use the search bar.

  5. Final Tip: HAVE FUN.
